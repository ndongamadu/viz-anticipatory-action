Trigger Definition

Food Insecurity

At least 20% population of one or more ADMIN1 regions be projected at IPC4+ OR At least 30% of ADMIN1 population be projected at IPC3+ AND with an increase by 5 percentage points compared to current state

Drought

At least 50% probability of below average rainfall from at least two seasonal rainfall forecasts OR Drought named as a driver of the deterioration of the situation in food security by FewsNet or Global IPC
